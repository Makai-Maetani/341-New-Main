DRY CANYON HIKING

Explore this 5.4-mile out-and-back trail near Lindon, Utah. Generally considered a challenging route, it takes an average of 4 h 37 min to complete. This is a popular trail for backpacking, camping, and hiking, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day. The best times to visit this trail are April through October. Dogs are welcome and may be off-leash in some areas.
